How to receive Bittorrent Airdrop (BTT) tokens

  1. Install the app.
  2. Hold at least 100 TRON on your Atomic address. For 1 TRX you will get about 0.01 BTT tokens.
    Ration is floating due to constant changes of the total amount of TRX (coin burn + mining).
  3. Wait for the distribution. Keep holding Tron till: 12:00:00 am on June 11th, 2019 UTC. At this time the snapshot of TRX blockchain will be taken to distribute BTT tokens.
  4. BTT will be distributed to your account in 72 hours after the snapshot time. The total amount of this airdrop is 990 million tokens.

What is BTT token?

BTT is a native token of Bittorrent, the largest file sharing protocol created by Bram Cohen in 2001. As the first decentralized tool. Bittorrent was created long before Bitcoin and became the most common file sharing protocol in the world. The BTT token serves as a reward method to connect content creators with the audience for earning and spending the currency without link-betweens. The tokens can be traded on exchanges and bought for enhancing the downloading speed. The Bittorrent client gets connected to the TRON blockchain as a means for more robust, censorship-free infrastructure.
The ambitious collaboration project aims to enable the mass adoption of crypto industry as a whole.
