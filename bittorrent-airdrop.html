BitTorrent Airdrop for TRON hodlers

Bittorrent Foundation has recently unleashed the details in the regards of their first airdrop which is set to be initiated for all TRON holders. Starting from February 11th, when the snapshot will be taken at the block height 6.6 million, the Foundation will airdrop 10,890,000,000 BitTorrent (BTT), corresponding to 1.1% of total circulating supply and 11,880,000,000 BTT over the next 12 months. By the mid 2020, the amount is planned for increasing up to 12,870,000,000 BTT that makes 1.3% of the total circulating supply. The additional 99,990,000,000 BTT will be distributed among the longtime holders.

What is BTT token?

BTT is a native token of Bittorrent, the largest file sharing protocol created by Bram Cohen in 2001. As the first decentralized tool. Bittorrent was created long before Bitcoin and became the most common file sharing protocol in the world. The BTT token serves as a reward method to connect content creators with the audience for earning and spending the currency without link-betweens. The tokens can be traded on exchanges and bought for enhancing the downloading speed. The Bittorrent client gets connected to the TRON blockchain as a means for more robust, censorship-free infrastructure.
The ambitious collaboration project aims to enable the mass adoption of crypto industry as a whole.

How to get BTT tokens?

  1. Install the app.
  2. Deposit any amount of Tron on your address. For 1 TRX you will get 0.11 BTT tokens.
  3. Wait for the distribution. The first one will start on February 11, 2019. Airdrops will continue every month.

    4. Note: Remember the first snapshot will be taken when Tron blockchain will reach 6.6M block height.
