TRON (TRX) Price Prediction for 2019

If to define TRX with professional terminology, this is a block chain of the protocol that has the Ethereum blockchain code, which means that the ecosystem is decentralized. In this case, the blockchain is adapted to the features of the entertainment market. Using the Tron platform, the audience can thus create their own individual gaming and entertainment projects, publish software, text, and graphics (visual) content, store it or transfer it for rent, for a fee, distribute and promote it through issuing and receiving assets.

TRON (TRX) Price Chart

The features of the system on the above advantages do not end at all, but can be continued without being limited to the following listing of advantages:

  • high transaction speed, which is otherwise called scaling;
  • decentralization, which means the absence of a single center, as the transfer and storage of information, as well as its anonymity;
  • security;
  • previously specified multicurrency;
  • global coverage in perspective.

In the meantime, this coverage extends mainly to the Asian entertainment market.

TRON Tokens

Currently, there are three types of these tokens. And they are used for various purposes. And one user can own all three varieties. Here they are:

  • TRONIX (TRX). The main currency of the system. For these tokens available to purchase any content available on the platform. They can also be exchanged for other cryptos or Fiat if there is a corresponding option. If necessary, TRX is bought on exchanges or cryptocurrency exchangers.
  • TRON Power (TP). It is impossible to buy or exchange these tokens. The only way to get them is to fasten the TRX coins on the platform. Then they are converted to TP. The resulting coins give a number of privileges in the system. For example, their owner has the right to vote for various changes on the platform.
  • TRC 20. With these tokens, third-party developers can create their own their own tokens based on the TRON network. The mechanism is similar to the principles of creating coins of the ERC20 standard on the Ethereum blockchain. The token emission is limited - a total of 100 billion coins were issued. Consequently, inflation is not worth it. But we still have to deal with the distribution of cryptos.

Forecast 2019 for TRON: what factors will affect the growth of TRX

According to experts, recently, the leading players in the entertainment market have seriously become interested in decentralized databases and are thinking about transferring their business to the blockchain. For this, they need ready-made solutions, and TRON seems to be the best option for that purpose. The founders of Tron were able to enlist the support of prominent Asian business circles. They invested their funds in the development of the platform: Tang Binsen, the creator of the famous computer game Clash of Kings, the founder of the OFO cycle-breeding company Dai Wei, the head of Lifan industrial concern Yin Minshan and other famous personalities.

Tron collaborates with major IT and blockchain companies such as Bitmain, ICO-365, Bixin, Gravityless, Openledger, which adds weight to the project within the crypto community. Just the other day, Justin Sun confirmed a partnership with the largest Chinese Internet provider Baidu Browser.

The 10-year development plan presented above gives an idea of the scale of the plan. Experts believe that if at least half of the items will be completed, the Tron has every chance of ousting Google Pay from App store.

In addition to focusing on the unlimited entertainment market, which in itself provides a great prospect, TRON has a number of other advantages that will not allow it to disappear even in conditions of a protracted crisis of the crypto industry.

Among them:

  • The ability to create on the basis of the platform own entertainment and game projects, publish graphic, program and text content, store it, distribute and sell for crypto;
  • The ability to exchange and make transactions with such assets as PTS, ETH, EOS, Qtum (in the future, the list of currencies is planned to be expanded);
  • High level of security.
  • The high speed of payment transactions;
  • Lack of a unified data transmission and storage center (decentralization);

As of October 28, 2018, the TRX rate is $ 0.0234, the market capitalization is $ 1.543 billion. With these indicators, the project holds 11th place in the Coinmarketcap rating.

No matter how long the current market fall, the revival and new stages of development of the Tron platform are ahead. At the beginning of the year, the most pessimistic forecast for TRON for 2019 was $ 5, today it is somewhat less. But the reason for this is not the problems of the project, but only the general situation in the crypto industry. The main part of sober-thinking and unbiased experts agree that in the 2019th TRX will cost between $ 30-50.

Thus, in a short time, the Tron team was able to prove the main thing: their brainchild is not just another bubble, but a promising long-playing project that will fully demonstrate its strengths in the next 10 years.
