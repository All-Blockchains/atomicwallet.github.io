Bitcoin Cash Price Prediction for 2019

Around the middle of July 2017, the developers agreed on the need to update the Bitcoin protocol using the SegWit2x technology (Segregated Witness). Such a solution was proposed by the Bitcoin Core development team, it was supported by the largest mining pools from China. Most miners agreed to update the protocol, which promised to improve the performance of the entire network. But some of the large mining companies supported the division.

Most supporters of this position are from China, it can be said that the Chinese miners caused the Bitcoin fork (by the way, not everyone agrees that this is a fork). But, in general, this is true. So, on August 1, the Bitcoin Cash crypto was born with a block size of 8 MB. Interestingly, the Chinese were the first to carry out operations with this cryptocurrency." A group of miners who did not like the option using SegWit2x decided to increase the block size of cryptocurrency from 1 MB to 8 MB," commented Charles Morris, a representative of NextBlock Global investment company.

Should you invest in Bitcoin Cash?

Soon after the creation, BCH set a record for the number of trading platforms, which added it to the list of trading pairs. Today, the coin is present on all the leading exchanges: Binance, Coinbase, Poloniex, Kraken, Bithumb, Bitstamp, GDAX, Bittrex. This makes the BCH accessible to a wide range of users and allows you to save on commissions (no extra conversions are required). The relatively young Crypstyx exchange, in the listing of which there is also a BCH, works directly with the euro. This means that you can replenish your wallet on the stock exchange with a simple bank transfer.

In the near future, developers are planning to introduce the function of depositing and withdrawing funds on plastic cards. Supporters of the BCH began to unite even before the Bitcoin network and the official release of the coin. The backbone of the community was formed of those who advocated hard forks. The advantages of the new platform, in which the processing of transactions took four times less time, could not help but notice other users. Even today, at a difficult time for the entire industry, the volume of BCH capitalization is approaching $ 10 billion. The Bitcoin Cash Network is developed in accordance with the scenario of the creator of the original Bitcoin. The developers plan to provide the technical ability to enjoy the benefits of cryptocurrency to all people on the globe. They plan to develop educational projects and create charitable foundations for third world countries. Opportunities for turning into a global cryptocurrency for daily payments.

What to expect in 2019?

In 2018 the news background for BCH was not too favorable. One of the first disturbing events was the delisting of BCH on the KuCoin and OKEx trading floors. Official reasons were not voiced, but according to rumors, couples with Bitcoin cash simply turned out to be unpopular.

July 15, one of the users published information that the activity of the developers of the BCH at the moment is actually zero. At the same time, the newsmaker stressed, the original Bitcoin team is intensively working on solving the problem of network scalability. He also focused on a significant reduction in the number of transactions Bitcoin Cash and reported that in one of the first days of July, users found a block weighing only 5 MB on the network. This fact can be explained both by the general decline in interest in cryptos against the background of long-term market stagnation and by the decline in popularity of the BCH itself.

Bitcoin Cash is not fully answered the expectations of investors. But according to the high level of capitalization, the big names of the developers and a large army of fans can hardly ever be forgotten in the coming years, and certainly, this will not happen in 2019. It is difficult to say what will happen in the long term because according to the fundamental technology the platform is inferior to the same Ethereum or EOS. Therefore, experts recommend considering the BCH as a tool for medium-term investments (for the next year and a half).
