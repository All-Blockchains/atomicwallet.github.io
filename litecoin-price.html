Litecoin Price Prediction for 2019

The popularity of Bitcoin gave birth to new cryptos and let them grow. Litecoin is one of the largest alternative cryptos and also one of the first alt-coins. Litecoin has the other way of transaction and mining – another as Bitcoin, of course, but the price and growth of the Litecoin still depend on its bigger brother.

Litecoin Price Chart

How it was

At the beginning of 2017 mining of the Litecoin was named as digital silver. The way of mining was perfectly simple, because of the blocks, that were easier to proceed. Home PC was enough for mining. These conditions were perfect for users because they were giving all the options for successful mining.

Litecoin Today

For the moment, the biggest part of the Litecoin is already mined. When we say the biggest part, we mean more than 80%. This result was reached at rather short times. Capitalization, by the way, was even more than 10 Mlrd. dollar, even though there were ups and downs of the Litecoin price.
Mining becomes more complicated and it seems like it will be a long time until the rest of the LTC will be mined.
And together with increasing of the number of coins, Litecoin will have even more possibilities for the capitalization.

Next Year rate theories

Litecoin could possibly be a rival for the Ethereum in 2019. That is related to the assumption, that the Litecoin is rather new on the market and not all the users take it serious enough. But the Litecoin price has good chances for going up in 2019. The positive marks for the growing of Litecoin price are also:

  • The high speed of creating the block
  • The big number of coins

But we should emphasize that the currency is somewhat unpredictable.

Perspectives

When the Litecoin was just released, it was added to some of the exchange resources. And the price went up so fast, no one expected. Now the Litecoin price depends on Bitcoin. At the same time, the speed of transactions, the already built-in SegWit, and the larger reward for mining indicate bright prospects for the coin. Expect a strong approach of silver Litecoin in 2019 to its gold counterpart in value is hardly worth it, but many predict a potential convergence of ETH and LTC courses. Now the ether overtakes LTC at a cost of more than 5 times. A number of crypto enthusiasts suggest that the difference in courses in 2019 will be no more than 3 times. Perspectives for the further independent development of LTC are based on the following characteristics of the coin:

  • high transaction speed;
  • a built-in protocol that increases the plasticity of the system and the speed of operations;
  • a large amount of reward for cloud mining.

What could possibly happen

Sam Quinn, the author of the online publication on cryptos with a focus on Ethereum analysis, said that he did not believe in serious competition between ETH and LTC. Although the right Litecoin called "digital silver" stays, and it will not be able to catch up with the broadcast in the near future. Mr. Quinn calls infancy the state in which the sphere of crypto is now located, so he draws an analogy with children's cries - what kind of "baby" cries louder, and that is the primary attention. Litecoin is still a quieter "child", but it has a chance to start early, rapid growth. For this reason, LTC in 2019 can behave unpredictably. In addition, in the last couple of years, the crypto has behaved positively, except for the depreciation of the course in January 2018.

Also, there were versions about the price of LTC for 2019 in the range of 350-500 dollars per coin. However, there are those who still predict the loss of their popularity by the Litecoin, which ultimately will lead to a fall in value, and a sharp drain on the coins, for this reason, will finally drop the course. The Economic Forecasting Agency analyzes not only real currencies, but also digital money. Specialists in this company suggest that there should be no surprising jumps in the cost of Litecoin in 2019. Moreover, the cost of LTC will fluctuate throughout the year, constantly rising and falling. In this case, the final result will show a significant increase in value - at the beginning of the year, Litecoin will cost 381, and by December, LTC will have to pay $ 505.

So, how will Litecoin be acting next year?

Litecoin is one of “unexpected” cryptos. It grew in 2013, then fell down, and then in 2017 started to grow again, with the high speed. Even though we can't really compare this type of growth with the other coins. It works like an Altcoin – gives the alternative and because of this has good price perspectives in 2019.

LTC price still didn’t reach 80$-100$ until the end of this year. That is why the Litecoin price could possibly grow up in 2019 and the LTC range will be between 200$ and 400$. Despite all the arguments in favor of Litecoin, some experts hypothesize that the popularity of this coin will come to naught, which in turn will lead to the collapse of the course and the exit of cryptocurrency from the market
