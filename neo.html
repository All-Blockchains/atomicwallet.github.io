Preface

Neo is a decentralized application platform created to store and send data in a distributive and trustless manner. The project was founded by the AntShares team (Da Hongfei and Erik Zhang) in 2014 and positioned as an “Open Network For Smart Economy” or “Chinese Ethereum”. We can also call it more Scalable Ethereum.

Neo combines Decentralized Storage and identification for individuals, organisations and institutions, smart contracts, atomic swaps support and their own virtual machine.

Neo blockchain can be a multi-tool in the world of crypto.

Neo Blockchain: Main Features

According to the Neo whitepaper, the project aims to build a Smart Economy consisted of the:

  1. Digital Assets
  2. Digital Identity
  3. Smart Contracts

Digital Assets can be used by organisations and individuals to make their own currencies or to convert real assets or good into digital assets. For example, to buy companies services or goods via NEO tokens (NEP-5).

Digital Identity is used for providing decentralized identification for individuals, companies and institutions. By that, the customers will be able to trust the service they use, blacklist malicious actors or to get access to a decentralized application or enhance their own decentralized experience. Just imagine that your ID is encrypted and you can make purchases by using your Digital Identification or receive access to your account providing your Digital ID.

Smart Contracts allows to make decentralized applications and execute operations of the certain terms are achieved.

Moreover, the project offers their own consensus algorithm in some way similar to the delegated proof-of-stake.

Delegated Byzantine Fault Tolerant (dBFT) consensus

Neo Blockchain is governed by the stakeholders. They hold funds and vote for a bookkeeper who generates a new block and validate transactions. Bookkeeper is a node (a computer that holds the whole blockchain history and make sure that transactions complies the full agreement with the rules). For such duties, the Bookkeeper receives rewards (transactions fees and reward for block generation). To become a bookkeeper, you need to put at least 10000 NEO in the escrow account and pay 1000 GAS + provide services and be elected. Then you can receive rewards for your duties.

Scalability

Average block creation time: 15-20 sec. The system can handle about 1000 transactions per second. And can achieve transaction speed equal to 10.000 transactions per second.

NeoX

NEO blockchain is also allowed to create and implement cross-chain Atomic Swaps technology to exchange one cryptocurrency to another without third-party need. You will be able to exchange Bitcoin to NEO.

NeoFS

Neo File Storage is a technology to store files right in the blockchain. This feature is created on the Distributive Hash Tables technology and will be used to store old blocks in it to solve the problem of free space for node-holders. Users and companies will be also able to store their data on blockchain and achieve access to them with Digital ID.

NeoVM

NeoVM is used to execute programs and smart companies on the blockchain. It’s a little bit similar to the Ethereum Virtual Machine but accept and execute multiple programming languages.

What is Neo Coin?

Neo Coin is the native asset of the NEO blockchain. It’s used as a digital asset, payment or investment instrument.

Neo also has their own token GAS. GAS is equal to Ethereum GAS and is used to make transactions, to pay for the smart-contracts execution and data storage. GAS in Neo blockchain created with block generation and paid as a reward to the bookkeeper and for the stakeholder. You can check the GAS calculation here.
