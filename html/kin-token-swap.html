Kin Token Swap

On 12, March, 2019 KIN has successfully undergone a token migration. Now all KIN holders can move their funds from the Ethereum Network to the native KIN blockchain.

Currently, the both versions of KIN are available in Atomic Wallet, so you can manage ERC20 KIN token, as well as the native KIN Coin, and easily swap one to another in 1:1 ratio.

Here is a short guide how to swap KIN ERC-20 Token to KIN Coin:

  1. Update Atomic Wallet to the latest version (1.38.5 or later)
    Download Atomic for Android, Windows, MacOS, Ubuntu, Debian, Fedora.
  2. Find KIN Coin in Atomic and copy wallet address (the mainnet address should start from “G”).
  3. Use one of instant exchanges:
    a) Changelly - https://kin.changelly.com/processing (registration needed)
    b) Coinswitch - https://coinswitch.co/kin-swap/ (without registration)
    c) Use any other swap partner from the official list - https://www.kin.org/migration.
  4. Paste your Atomic Wallet Kin address into the corresponding field. Leave the “memo” empty. You don’t need one if you use Atomic Wallet.
  5. Send your ERC20 KIN token to the address provided by the partner.

    Or you can use the form below to proceed with swap.

What is Kin Project?

Kin was created in 2017 to use the power of blockchain and crypto to create a new ecosystem that puts developers, users, and content creators on the same team (Visit Kin Website). By aligning everyone’s interests, Kin gets rid of us vs. them and replaces it with a whole new way to think about value that let’s everyone succeed together by focusing on user engagement, rather than by turning users into products.

App developers have the chance to focus on creating amazing user experiences, users get to enjoy the best apps without worrying about their data being stolen, and everyone gets to be recognized and fairly compensated for the value they create. This isn’t just a new way of doing business, it’s a new way of thinking that puts people at the center while focusing on creativity, experience, and connection.

What is Kin Coin

Kin empowers consumer app developers to create deep engagement, increase retention, and drive sustainable revenue and growth as part of a comprehensive ecosystem in which everyone wins together. With easy-to-use blockchain tools and the security and speed of the Kin Blockchain, developers are able to harness the power of cryptocurrency without all the backend complexities of the technology.
Today, millions of consumers already have access to Kin through popular apps, like Kik, Perfect365, Tapatalk & Madlipz
