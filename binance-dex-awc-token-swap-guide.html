Guide to Swap Atomic Wallet Token (AWC)

Atomic Wallet’s native AWC token is migrating from the Ethereum to Binance chain. 50.000.000 AWC or 50% of token supply are issued in BEP2 token standard.
In this step-by-step guide you’ll learn how to swap your ERC20-AWC tokens to BEP2-AWC tokens and back without hassle!

The swap process is powered by one of our exchange partners ChangeNOW and takes just a couple of minutes.

  1. Log on to https://changenow.io/swap/atomic and input the amount of AWC to swap. Click “Swap”.

  2. Open your Atomic Wallet and find the address of AWC-BNB token. Note that it starts with ‘bnb’!

  3. Paste the address in ChangeNOW’s form. Leave “Memo” empty. Go next.

  4. Now check everything. Is that okay? Confirm the transaction, if so.

  5. Now you need to send the amount indicated to ChangeNOW’s address. Go to your Atomic Wallet, find your AWC ERC20 and send the tokens.

  6. Let the magic happen! Wait several minutes.

  7. Tada! Check your wallet in the blockchain and your AWC BNB wallet!

Any troubles with the swap? Don’t worry! Contact support@atomicwallet.io to get help.
