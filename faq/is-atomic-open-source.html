Is Atomic Wallet fully open sourced?

Atomic Wallet is a community-driven product that utilized open sourced libraries, block explorers and other important elements. By combining all these elements, we put a lot of effort and time to provide a ready-made product customers can rely on. As well-experienced crypto enthusiasts, we know how crucial security is and the risks any cryptocurrency holder is exposed. For that reason, we aim to protect the users of Atomic Wallet and the product itself from scams and bootleg applications by keeping some part of our development in secret.
