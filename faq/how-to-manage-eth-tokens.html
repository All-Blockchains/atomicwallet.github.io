How to manage ETH (ERC20) tokens in Atomic Wallet?

Atomic Wallet supports ANY ERC20 tokens. If you haven't found your token in the list just click on the "Add token" button.

To add your token just paste token contract address. All other data (Full name, Short Name and Decimals of precision) will be updated automatically.

To find your token Contact Address:

  • Go to https://etherscan.io and make a search for your token name.
  • Copy the Contract Address and paste it into the corresponding field in Atomic Wallet.

All other information will be filled automatically. Now you have your Custom Token in Atomic Wallet.

Once attached to your Ethereum address, you can send, store or receive this token in Atomic Wallet.

Please don't forget that all ERC20 tokens are based on Ethereum. To manage such assets you will need to have at least a small amount of ETH in your wallet.
