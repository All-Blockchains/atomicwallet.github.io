F.A.Q.
What is Atomic Wallet?
Atomic Wallet is a non-custodial solution for the overwhelming majority of your cryptocurrency. You can secure and manage your digital assets and tokens in one place, whether it is on your computer or smartphone.
What is AWC?
AWC (Atomic Wallet Coin) is a utility ERC20 token that serves as fuel for extra features, rewards, bounty program and other partnership privileges.
How fees are caclulated in Atomic Wallet?
Atomic Wallet utilizes dynamic network fees that are calculated automatically, depending on the current state of a blockchain.
What are Private Keys?
Private keys are the essential part of your wallet that gives you full control over your funds. Whenever you send or receive crypto, the transaction gets assigned with a private key.
Only you control your private keys
Atomic Wallet gives you full control over your your private keys. Only you have access to your funds and only you controls your coins, tokens and transactions on your device.

What is the difference between swaps and exchanges
Atomic Wallet is a unique wallet that provides you with a variety of exchange options, such as Instant Exchange and Atomic Swaps.
How to buy Cryptocurrency in Atomic?
In Atomic Wallet, you can buy BTC, LTC, ETH, XRP and BCH right within the wallet interface. All fiat-to-crypto transactions are powered by Simplex, the largest and trustful bank card operator.
How to exchange Cryptocurrency in Atomic?
In Atomic Wallet, you can exchange over 60 cryptocurrencies without using external exchange websites (do not confuse with Atomic Swaps).
How to use Atomic Swaps?
In Atomic Wallet, you can swap Bitcoin, Litecoin and QTUM (in the future, this feature will be available for Ethereum and other altcoins as well).
