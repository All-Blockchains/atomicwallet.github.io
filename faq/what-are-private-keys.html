What are Private Keys?

A private key is like a password to a digital bank account. It is an essential part of your wallet that gives you full control over your funds. Whenever you send or receive crypto, the transaction gets assigned with a private key. In the case of custodial wallets, transactions are assigned automatically by wallet operators. In Atomic Wallet, you need to sign each transaction with a private key manually. To do that, you need to enter your password before sending money.

How to Export Private Keys?

You can look up your private keys in Settings -> Private Keys tab.

To export your private keys go to "Settings" -> "Private keys" and Insert your password.

You will see the list of private keys. To copy your private key -> just click on it.

Then paste it in any other wallet that supports the coin that you're want to export.
