How do I use Atomic Swaps in Atomic Wallet?

In Atomic Wallet, you can swap Bitcoin, Litecoin and QTUM (in the future, this feature will be available for Ethereum and other altcoins as well). To do that, go to the “Atomic Swap” section. In the “Markets” tab, you’ll see available orders, the rates at which you can swap your crypto. These rates are updated on a regular basis from coinmarketcap.com. You can also place your own order and wait someone to close the order. Just input the amount to swap and set the margin. Once set, the rate gets locked until the order is complete, and you get your money. You pay only for a network fee.
