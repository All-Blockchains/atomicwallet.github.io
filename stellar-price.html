Stellar Price Prediction for 2019

Stellar is based on open code from XRP, so it inherited many features from the latter. This is a decentralized protocol for financial transactions, which can be used to send money anywhere in the world, in any currency pair. For example, the protocol supports sending money in dollars and receiving in euros. The source code of all components is open, the use is absolutely free. Stellar, like XRP, aims to bridge the gap between cryptos and fiat money, making it easy to transfer money between them. In essence, this is a distributed global currency exchange. But the most interesting thing is that the creators give out free currency to anyone. You can also get your part.

How Stellar works

For those familiar with Ripple, there’s almost nothing new. There is a currency called XLM (also called Stellar), which is the native currency of the Stellar network. The network is based on Ledger, a shared ledger. Ledger stores account balances and transaction history, similar to the Blockchain tree in the Bitcoin system. Total created 100 billion XLM. This volume will increase by 1% per year. To protect Ledger from creating a large number of accounts, a minimum balance of 50 XLM is set on each Stellar account. This is a small amount for regular users, but it does not allow attackers to overload the network with spam. When processing a transaction, 0.00001 XLM is charged from the sender. This is not a commission charged for anyone's benefit, XLM is simply written off and ceases to exist. This protects the network from "transactional spam" (creating a large number of transactions). XLM can be instantly transferred to any account without any payment for transfer, it cannot be devalued since it exists in limited quantities. Stellar support any currency. Users are not required to use XLM as a medium of exchange or savings.

Stellar distributed currency exchange allows users to trade without the need to hire a broker or resort to third-party currency exchange. Anyone can place offers or offers in the Ledger database, and the network will find the most effective way to establish contact between the parties to the transaction. For example, you keep money in an account in US dollars and send a payment to a seller who accepts payments only in euros. The Stellar network itself “converts” currencies by routing orders through a network of market makers who compete to earn bid / ask spreads.

XLM is convenient to use as an intermediate currency. If two counterparties cannot find a common currency/combination of gateways, they can use XLM as a neutral currency without any counterparty risks. For example, Angie prefers US dollars, and Peter prefers euros. If they are unable to find a suitable market maker working with EUR / USD to complete their transaction, they can conveniently exchange their preferred currency for XLM and XLM for their preferred currency in order to be able to trade with each other.

The latest Stellar news, rate change in 2018

The graph of the stellar course for the current year shows calm dynamics, except for two growth waves. The first came at the beginning of January and showed a maximum of $ 0.88. By the time, it coincided with the addition of stellar to the list of trading pairs on the OKEx exchange, although the general rise in the market also played a role. Then the course smoothly descended over four months, and only in May again began to show an upward trend. The second spurt occurred in July against the background of several resonant news at once: listing on Coinbase, obtaining Sharia certification from the Bank of Bahrain, the start of the work of the technology giant IBM with the Stronghold bank on a new stable coin based on the Stellar platform.
