How to buy Ethereum with Credit Card

You can buy Ethereum with VISA/MasterCard right in the interface of Atomic Wallet and convert your USD or EUR to ETH.

Even if you have a JPY, CAD or any other currency card, those currencies are automatically converted to either EUR or USD, depending on your choice. It’s quite easy to use and doesn’t require specific tech skills.


How it works?

We are partnered with the largest EU licensed card processing company, Simplex. When you make a request to buy cryptos, you need to pass the verification process. You do that only once. Your issuer bank will hold the money, and they will be processed by Simplex, in less than 24 hours you will receive crypto on your address. If the operation is declined, you will receive a full refund.

Rates and Security

The fees are fixed at 7%. Each operation is fully conducted by Simplex, an EU licensed card processing company. You choose an amount and currency to buy and verify your ID. Then your bank holds funds until Simplex approves the request and makes an exchange. As a rule, you receive crypto in 24 hours.
