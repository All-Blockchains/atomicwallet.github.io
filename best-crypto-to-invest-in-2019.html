Best cryptocurrencies to invest in 2019

Here is the time to write an article, in which we will take a look at the perspectives of different cryptos in 2019. The year 2018 was complicated for all the specialists that are working with cryptos – from developers till the platform operators and also the investors. What should we be ready for in 2019?

What Bitcoin Price is to expect?

We think, that the safest situation is with the Bitcoin, the top crypto of all time. First, last year it was already coming out of dangerous situations. Second, BTC is the exactly that instrument, what are the example for all the developments and all the cryptos and their infrastructure.

Even more, crypto-community is waiting, that in 2019 will the regular measures are taken, and the investment platforms, that would help the cryptos to grow. On the basis of this Bitcoin is still the most perspective cryptos on the market.

Actual price on the market: After a historic high of $ 1,200 in December 2013, over the next 13 months, Bitcoin fell by more than 87% and found a bottom of $ 150 in January 2015. Now, with a maximum of $ 20,000, an 87% drop will result in $ 2,500. Maybe the bottom will be reached in January 2019.

What Ethereum Price is to expect?

As for the second best cryptocurrency to invest in, the Ethereum rate keeps dropping. In comparison with other assets, Ethereum’s falling sharply. Currently, Ethereum is being traded at around 118 USD, which is almost 8 times lower than a year ago. Some analytics expect Ethereum to surpass the mark below 100$. These expectations, however, may turn out to be far from the truth, because the upcoming Constantinople hardfork may influence the Ethereum price in 2019. From that point, Ethereum still seems to be appealing for crypto investments.

It was announced that the Coin Shuffling technology will be integrated into the BTC network. It should add to make the level of protection of user data higher. Their coins will be mixed. Because of this, it will be harder to watch the movement of the actives in the net. This decision will have no influence on the transaction prices.

Actual price on the market: On Friday, December 21, the market share of Bitcoin fell to 53%, over the last week this figure fell by 2%. Since December 15, the first cryptocurrency has risen in price by 28%: from $ 3,200 to $ 4,100. During the same period of time, some Altcoins increased in price more - market capitalization increased by 34%, to $ 134 billion.

Invest in XRP (Ripple)?

The second by the popularity crypto in 2019 will possibly be the XRP coin, that was developed by the Ripple Labs. We should notice that on the falling market XRP took the 2 places from Ethereum.

Ripple is now developing by the guidance of the world banks. That means that it is in the biggest field comparing to the other cryptos. That is why the growth of XRP could be faster than by the other cryptos on the market.

Invest in Stellar XLM?

Stellar is the decentralized platform for high-speed cheap transactions. It is the fork of the Ripple platform. It is a perspective because of the different factors. Obviously, the 2018th year turned out to be extremely unfortunate, bringing down the price tag on many cryptocurrencies. Bitcoin itself has lost more than 80% of its value.

In this situation, Stellar had no other options than to dive with the rest of the market. However, it is worth remembering that giants like IBM do not enter into cooperation and do not invest in projects without a future. The fact that IBM turned to this particular agent practically guarantees us that Stellar has a good future. Considering all this, now it is best for investors to continue dodging the coin, instead of merging all their XLMs due to the low price tag.

Today, cryptocurrencies are experiencing a real storm, which, one way or another, is destined to come to an end. Not all projects will be able to survive it, but those who remain afloat will continue to thrive. It is possible that this will happen even sooner than we expect. Many experts tend to believe that very soon, many organizations will begin to invest in the crypto, and the 2019th year will be the beginning of the golden age of cryptocurrencies.
