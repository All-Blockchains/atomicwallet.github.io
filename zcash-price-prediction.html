What is ZCash?

Zcash (ZEC) was developed by an American Colorado-based computer security specialist Zooko Wilcox-O'Hearn in 2016. Known for his ascetic lifestyle, the cypherpunk designed a privacy-focused blockchain with a market capitalization of more than $350 million. Zcash uses particular cryptography which protects the security of its user and doesn’t disclose any information on the transaction without the user’s prior consent. Such anonymity is provided by the proof of construction protocol named zk-snark. In contrast to Bitcoin which requires verification of every single transaction, Zcash keeps both users' balance and the number of transactions in private.

ZEC has a total supply of 21 million coins which are expected to be mined by 2150. The Zcash news report that the platform processes one block for 2,5 minutes which is 4 times faster than Bitcoin.

The capacity of Zcash to maintain the privacy of transactions has attracted numerous customers and established partnerships with different institutions around the globe. Moreover, the company’s protocol has recently been replicated by Ethereum blockchain, as it is reported on the Zcash news.

To date, ZEC has risen to the 20th place in spite of its market cap being reduced by an amount of $2.3 billion. The rise in popularity is attributable to society’s growing interest in privacy concerns which are met and respected by the Zcash company. Zcash price prediction 2019

Despite the fact that 2018 saw a huge collapse of numerous coins including ZEC, the latter has been maintaining stability over the past months. Like other coins, Zcash coin price is mostly determined by the market sentiment which, luckily, stays positive towards the coin. Even though it is hard to predict how public perception might change over time, we still may explore what factors it might be influenced by.

First of all, last September saw an upgrade of Zcash which made it compatible with far more devices than it was before, according to the Zcash news. Sapling, the long-awaited update, is aimed at making transactions faster and easier while the privacy of users will still be protected. According to Zcash, the time for processing a transaction will be six times less than before while a transaction itself will take a hundred times less memory space. Ever since the launch of Sapling, many users reported better performance of the platform.

Secondly, Zcash price growth may be driven by the coin’s integration into mobile wallets, as mobile payments are gradually gaining in popularity. With a bigger audience, the company is likely to see the value of its coin go up.

Briefly, Zcash predictions are rather optimistic, as experts show belief in the coin’s potential. Only time will tell whether Zcash coin price will meet the public expectations.

Another important fact that might positively influence the value of the currency is that the number of coins is limited, and the more ZEC is mined, the higher is the price for the rest of the coins. The company plans to maintain miners interest in ZEC by increasing their reward by one fourth and expects the coin’s price to hit $700. One way or another, the whole crypto market is expected to be up by 2020 which cannot but contribute positively to Zcash value.
