Atomic Wallet Token (AWC) migration to Binance chain

  • 50.000.000 AWC on Ethereum blockchain (ERC20) will be burned.
  • 50.000.000 AWC or 50% of token supply will be issued on the Binance Chain - BEP2 token standard.

Total AWC supply will remain the same as current. You’ll be able to swap AWC between the chains: from ERC-20 token to BEP2 token. We will follow up on the Binance Chain development for a possibility of full migration to the BEP2 standard.

How to swap AWC from ERC20 to Binance Chain?

For your convenience, we’ll arrange token swap via the Atomic Wallet app, as well as via special web service. The swap will be powered by one of our exchange partners - ChangeNOW. Check our step-by-step guide on how to make a swap.

AWC trading on Binance DEX

You will be able to trade AWC on Binance DEX, once the token is listed. All trades will be handled by the AWC/BNB trading pair in a decentralized manner. Follow our news for the updates and trading guides.
