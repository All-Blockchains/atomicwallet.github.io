What is VeChain Foundation?

The VeChain Foundation is a non-profit organization based in Singapore. It is one of the leading public blockchain platforms at the enterprise level.

The project was founded and started by Sunny Lu in 2015. He is ex CIO of Louis Vuitton in China and worked in Fortune 500 companies as IT-executive for over 13 years. VeChain has accumulated great experience in an ever-expanding list of industries.

The main goal of VeChain is to connect blockchain technology to the real world. It provides a comprehensive management structure, reliable economic model and advanced IoT integration in the real-world applications.

What is IoT technology?

IoT (Internet of Things) is a system of interrelated computing devices, mechanical and digital machines, objects, living creatures or people that are provided with unique identifiers (UIDs). It provides opportunity to transmit data over the network without requiring a "human to computer" interaction.

The IoT system consists of smart devices with network support that use built-in processors, sensors and communication equipment to accumulate, send and process the data they acquire in their environment.

IoT devices share their data by connecting to an IoT gateway. Sometimes the devices cooperate with other related devices and affect the information they receive from each other. Devices perform most of the work without human involvement, but people can interact with devices to set them up or access data, for example.
