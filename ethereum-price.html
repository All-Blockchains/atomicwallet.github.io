Ethereum price prediction in 2019

Ethereum Value

The analytics continue to talk about the falling of a price of Ethereum. Actually, the ETH price goes down faster, then the price of the other top tokens. This fact has surprised the experts and the question, where Ethereum price is heading is now opened.

Ethereum price today

It seems like ETH got a goal: to reach the center of Earth. Ethereum prices are falling and at this moment the ETH price was around 130$. We should say that at the beginning of a year Ethereum price was 1200$. From these facts comes that the crypto lost more than 90% of the original value.


Ethereum Price Prediction 2019

There is a risk for Ethereum price to go even lower in 2019. The next support area for the crypto was at the 100$ position. And as you can see, it can easily come down to 91$. The psychological and digital support is important. The last time Ethereum being so low was in 2017 and that time the digital active formed the support of 91$.

However, during the day of December 17, the value of the digital money market increased sharply: in an hour it grew by $4 billion and now amounts to $109 billion. Most digital money went up in a short period of time: the price of Bitcoin rose by 5%, XRP by 4%, Stellar by 6%, EOS - by 11%. The average market rate of Ethereum increased by 4%, to $90. According to experts, the coin has overcome the resistance level of $86, in the near future, it can rise in price to $92. If the crypto successfully overcomes this zone, its value will be able to reach $95 and $100 in a short period of time. Otherwise, the asset's rate will fall to $85 and $83, the expert concluded.

The falling of the ETH price explains the skepticism of some investors – now we see that it is more a possibility than an imagination for Ethereum to drown into Double Digits in 2019. The situation on the market is hard to the prognosis for now, because of all these high-speed falls and coming up of ETH price.

Ethereum Price Forecast

The forecast for Ethereum depends on the events planned by the network developers for 2019 and announced by Vitalik Buterin in the spring of this year. After the planned hardfork, the protocol will be changed: switching from PoW (proof of work) to the distribution of the share Proof-of-Stake (Casper), which will provide a threefold increase in throughput, increase the speed of payment transactions, reduce the cost of auxiliary internal currency Gas, which users pay for conducting transactions. With the introduction of PoS, the complexity of the network will increase, which will certainly entail an increase in the eth rate.

Note: Ethereum Constantinople Hardfork has been postponed due to security reasons. It will be deployed on February 27, 2019.

Ethereum Coin Price

Ethereum blockchain is one of the most useful blockchains on the market. The current Ethereum price fell below 100$ and now we need a prognosis for 2019. Actually, it is hard to say for sure how the situation will turn. To more accurately answer the question about Ethereum price prediction, you need to look at the latest news.

For ETH price, it’s noteworthy that:

  • Regulators are getting tougher to treat cryptocurrencies. Starting from the appearance of control at the state level and up to a total ban on any actions by cryptocurrencies in the country.
  • The overall decline in the cryptocurrency market. Recently, the whole market has fallen.
  • Large trading platforms impose bans on creating advertising for any cryptocurrencies or their withdrawal to ICO. For example, Google and Facebook.

To better understand this, you can compare Ethereum rate in 2017 - $ 1,400, and the rate of 2018 - $ 400 for March. Such a fall in the course causes serious blows to the capitalization of the project. But it does not hurt so much the other altcoins. The founder of the platform, Vitalik Buterin, lost about $ 300 million in a fall.

Ethereum News: Ban on ICO in China

The ban on ICO in China has affected Ethereum, as well. Indeed, in this country, there was a considerable number Ether users.

That in this way the Chinese authorities want to cut off all foreign projects from the country, giving more chances for the development of their NEO. Some experts suggest that the ban is temporary.

What to do if the Ethereum course falls: sell or hodl?

The most important thing to do in the event of a fall in the rate of Ethereum is not to start a panic, not to try to sell as soon as possible all your coins for the price offered by the market.
